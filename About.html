About this amazing waffle hotspot

This website was made for people who love waffles and want a place where they can browse products to further enhance their enjoyment, experience and representation of them.

There are some people who believe that pancakes are in fact the superior syrup and butter delivery system.
They are wrong.
It is an absolute fact that waffles are indeed the quintessential food, and the facts are there to support it.

The Facts:

  • They are ergonomically designed to hold the absolutely massive amounts of syrup and butter you are dumping on top of them.

  • Waffles do not get soggy nearly as fast. The perfect nook and cranny design also acts like a crust preventing mass amounts of syrup from soaking in and drenching your perfect breakfast. Much like when Anakin Skywalker and Obi-wan Kenobi were using the force create a barrier and push each other away.

  • Unlike their floppy, inferior cousin, waffles are way more portable and versatile. With their superior durability and rigidity you can take a waffle wherever, whenever.

  • Waffles can and have been be used to make and inspire many different types of treats and meals. Chicken and waffles, Waffle fries, Waffle cones, Waffle sandwiches. Have you ever seen a pancake sandwich? No, because they are too weak and cant handle the pressure.
